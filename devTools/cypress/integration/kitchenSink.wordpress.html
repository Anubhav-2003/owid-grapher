Redirect + override or redirect vs override?

When a link is redirected, any override will continue to take over, even if the destination of the link has a title. This means the title of the prominent link might end up out of sync with the title of the target (either article, grapher or explorer). For instance, if a redirect from /grapher/old-chart to /grapher/new-chart is added, any prominent link to /grapher/old-chart will be rewritten as /grapher/new-chart. However, their respective title will only automatically change to "New chart" for those prominent links with a blank title. The ones using a title override will continue to show that title. Conclusion: only use title (and image) overrides when absolutely necessary.
